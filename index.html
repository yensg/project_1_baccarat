BACCARAT

Step 1: Select Token to add to White Button.

Step 1A: Select Add + or Minus - Buttons to manipulate.

Step 2: Click on White Button to play, or Fold to not play.

Step 3: Click on Hit to have the 3rd Card, or Stay to continue with 2 Cards.


Winning Conditions: First digit of the sum of all your final cards on hand must be bigger than dealers'.
Example: J, 7, 5 = 12: First digit of the sum of all your final cards on hand is 2.
First digit must be > 5. 9 is the highest value.

Each game contains 2 waves: First wave 2 Cards.
Second wave to choose Hit to get third card, or Stay with 2 Cards.
